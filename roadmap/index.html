Critical Path Dependencies

These items must be completed on schedule as delays will cascade through subsequent phases.

‚ö†Ô∏è Priority Timeline Items

  • IRCC Express Entry Category Approval (6 months): Federal regulatory process. Begin Q2 2026, complete Q4 2026.
  • Provincial Stream Regulation Amendments (4-8 months): Each province requires legislative/regulatory changes. Stagger to avoid bottlenecks.
  • Indigenous Guardian Network Partnership Agreement (12-18 months): Nation-to-nation negotiations cannot be rushed. Begin immediately.
  • Credential Recognition System Integration (8-12 months): WES/IQAS partnership, SER CERP pathway, ECO Canada coordination.
  • Monitoring Platform Development (12 months): GIS integration, Restor.eco connection, RADARSAT data feeds. Parallel track to avoid delays.
  • Settlement Service Provider Contracting (6 months per region): RFP process, evaluation, contract execution. Regional variation significant.

This roadmap establishes clear timelines, responsibilities, and milestones for transparent program implementation. All stakeholders accountable for designated deliverables. Regular public reporting (quarterly minimum) ensures accountability. Independent evaluation at pilot conclusion prevents continuation of ineffective programs.
